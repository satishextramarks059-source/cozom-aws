Meet Our Leadership Team

The passionate minds behind COZOM – driving healthcare innovation with AI and compassion.

Satish Maurya

Founder & CEO

satish@cozom.in

About

Passionate entrepreneur and health-tech innovator, leading COZOM to transform healthcare accessibility through artificial intelligence. He is the visionary behind COZOM — an AI-powered platform that connects users from symptoms to the right doctor in a simple, multilingual, and intelligent way.

Background

With a strong foundation in Computer Science and IT, Satish combines technical understanding with real-world problem-solving. He identified a major gap in early disease detection and doctor accessibility in India, inspiring him to build a scalable solution that empowers users to make informed health decisions anytime, anywhere.

Vision

To make quality healthcare accessible to every Indian, regardless of their location or economic status, through intelligent technology and compassionate service.
